Brief Biography

I was born September 22nd, 1985, in Boston, Massachusetts. Being raised in Boston gave me
a thick Bostonian accent up until my adult years where I began moving around the country.
I grew up with best friend Matt who for most of my life has had the nickname Burgah Boy.
BB and I are still best friends and my experiences with him shaped who I am today.

From a young age, I was very interested in all things media. When I was 8, BB and I split our
cash that we'd saved for over 5 months to buy an SNES. It was the first video game console
we'd ever owned, and I've loved video games ever since. When I was 10 I won a local
Blockbuster Video Game Championship, one of the greatest achievements of my life.

On top of video games, filmmaking and entertainment quickly became my passions. While
pursuing a communications degree in my early 20s, I found myself making content for YouTube.
One of my earlier my groundbreaking videos was a short film called H2O in which a man is
trapped in his bathtub for weeks and is forced to take drastic measures to survive. I've since
gained tens hundreds of thousands of subscribers and continue to grow my channel to this day.

Career Goals

As mentioned in my biography, filmmaking and entertainment continue to be my passion and I
plan to keep bringing new content to the world. YouTube has always been an amazing platform
for sharing my vision, but I plan on moving beyond it for bigger and better projects.

I currently live in Las Vegas, known for its variety of entertainment and the perfect city for
fulfilling my plans of branching into show business. Eventually, my dream is to have the
opportunity to create a full feature film.
